    Products

    Toolie

    Have you ever found yourself in need of tools to complete a DIY project or fix in your home, only to realize you don't have the necessary tools? Buying tools can be expensive, and renting from big corporate companies may come with steep fees and no guarantee of availability. That's where Toolie comes in. We've created an app for the community to share and rent tools, so you don't have to buy them. We offer a collection of smaller general tools, including wrenches, drills, dremels, and more. If we don't have a tool you need, we outsource rentals from the community. For example, if you need a power washer for a day, you can request to rent one, and we'll post an advertisement on your behalf.
    We also welcome donations of tools you no longer need. And if you're looking to sell your tools or equipment, we're happy to work out an agreement to take them off your hands and help others in the community. At Toolie, we're all about making tools accessible and affordable for everyone, while fostering a sense of community and collaboration.
